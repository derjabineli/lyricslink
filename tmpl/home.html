LyricLink is a powerful lyric-sharing platform designed to streamline the way worship leaders share lyrics.

By integrating with Planning Center, LyricLink allows users to effortlessly create, format, and share lyric sheets via a simple URL. Whether you're leading a worship set, coordinating with a band, or organizing lyrics for an event, LyricLink makes distribution seamless and accessible

Our mission is to remove the hassle of formatting and distribution, so you can focus on worshipping with your congregation.
